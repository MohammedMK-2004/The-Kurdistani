What is the UKH MUSIC Band?

The UKH Music Band is a passionate group of students from the University of Kurdistan Hewlêr, united by their love for music. We perform a variety of genres and aim to bring joy and inspiration to our university community through live performances and creative collaborations.

Background

Established in 2019 by Maestro Dr. Hiwa Tallat Arif, The UKH Music Band has attended numerous events hosted by the University of Kurdistan Hewlêr. The band aims to share incredible musical experiences with the entire university and help the community become musically more advanced.
